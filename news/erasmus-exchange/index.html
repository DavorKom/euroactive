Erasmus+ Youth Exchange 2025: Building Bridges in Split

Published: October 10, 2025

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our next major **Erasmus+ Youth Exchange program**, scheduled for the summer of 2025 in the beautiful coastal city of Split, Croatia. This year's theme is **"Digital Diplomacy and Sustainable Futures."** The program aims to gather 50 young participants from across Europe to engage in workshops, cultural activities, and collaborative projects focusing on leveraging digital tools for environmental advocacy and international communication.

The exchange is a powerful non-formal learning experience designed to equip participants with essential skills in leadership, intercultural dialogue, and project management. Over the course of seven days, participants will work in international teams, developing solutions to local challenges and learning how to effectively communicate their ideas to a global audience.

Applications will open on November 1st. We encourage all interested youth between the ages of 18 and 30 to prepare their motivational letters. Previous participants have described the Euroactive Croatia youth exchanges as transformative, fostering lifelong friendships and opening doors to future European collaboration projects. Don't miss this opportunity to be part of an inspiring movement!
