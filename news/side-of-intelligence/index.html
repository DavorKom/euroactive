SIDE of Intelligence 2.0 - Artificial Intelligence in Youth Work

Published: March 4, 2026

In January 2026, our members Anamarija and Davor had the opportunity to participate in the Erasmus+ training course SIDE of Intelligence 2.0, which took place in Side, Turkey, from 19. to 27. January.

The training was organised by the Romanian association EUROACTIVE and brought together participants from Croatia, Romania, Spain, Italy, Serbia, Slovakia, Bulgaria, Sweden, Lithuania, Turkey and North Macedonia. This international environment created a dynamic space for exchange, cooperation and mutual learning.

The programme focused on networking and on developing knowledge about artificial intelligence tools and their practical application in the everyday work of organisations working with young people. Participants explored AI tools, AI agents and large language models, while also discussing challenges and possible solutions in NGO work. Through interactive sessions and joint activities, they exchanged experiences, strengthened existing partnerships and created opportunities for future collaboration.

Upon returning home, Anamarija and Davor emphasised that the training significantly increased their motivation to integrate AI tools into their work, helped clarify many previously open questions and broadened their understanding of the role of artificial intelligence in youth work.

We are returning with new knowledge, fresh ideas and valuable contacts that we look forward to transforming into new projects and opportunities for young people.
