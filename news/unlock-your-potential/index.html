Unlock Your Potential: Developing Soft Skills for Employability

Published: September 2, 2025

In August 2025, the association Euroactive Croatia hosted its first Erasmus+ youth exchange project entitled Unlock Your Potential: Developing Soft Skills for Employability.

From 2. to 9. August, 31 young people from Croatia, Greece, Hungary, Romania and Spain gathered in Orahovica. The main objective of this Erasmus+ youth exchanges was to develop soft skills in order to increase young people’s competitiveness on the labour market. Through a series of workshops, participants worked on skills such as non-violent communication, assertiveness, negotiation, conflict resolution and active listening.

The project was about much more than just learning. We spent a lot of time socializing, exploring Orahovica, and getting to know each other’s cultures. We discovered the town through a fun treasure hunt, took trips to Ružica Grad and the lake, and even hosted 'Orahovision,' which was our very own version of Eurovision. One of the best parts was the intercultural evening, where everyone shared their traditional food, dances, and customs.

As a result of the exchange, participants gained greater confidence in their job search, developed valuable skills for their professional future, and formed friendships which, as they say, will last a lifetime.

For young people seeking projects that are both educational and inspiring, Erasmus+ youth exchanges offer a unique opportunity. It is important to note that travel, food and accommodation costs in these projects are covered by the ERASMUS+ programme and co-financed by the European Union.

Euroactive Croatia opens the door to new experiences. Information about current and upcoming projects is regularly published on our social media channels (Facebook: Euroactive Hrvatska and Instagram: @euroactive_hrvatska).
