APPETISER - An introduction to international youth work

Published: November 27, 2025

In October, Euroactive Croatia had the opportunity to take part in a training course in Rome entitled APPETISER – An Introduction to International Youth Work, aimed primarily at youth workers active in the field of international youth engagement.

From 10 to 14 October, 18 youth workers from 10 different countries gathered in the capital of Italy to exchange experiences, strengthen their competences and deepen their understanding of international youth work. The training was organised by the Italian National Agency in cooperation with the Bulgarian National Agency and focused on acquiring essential knowledge of the Erasmus+ programme, with particular emphasis on youth mobility, the European Solidarity Corps (ESC) and methods of non-formal learning.

The programme was led by experienced youth workers and representatives of organisations with extensive experience in the field, ensuring a high-quality learning process. Participants shared knowledge, good practices and challenges from their local contexts, while informal networking moments also provided space to connect and explore potential future cooperation.

During the activity, participants stayed at the Basilica Hotel in Rome. Alongside the educational programme, time was set aside to experience the city itself. Participants visited some of Rome’s most iconic landmarks, including the Colosseum, Trevi Fountain, the Pantheon and Vatican City, while also enjoying authentic Italian cuisine.
