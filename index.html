ABOUT US

Euroactive Croatia is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering youth through education, mobility, and active citizenship.


Our mission is to provide young people with opportunities to develop personal and professional skills, foster intercultural understanding, and contribute positively to their local communities. We believe in the power of non-formal education to bridge gaps and create a more inclusive society for everyone.
